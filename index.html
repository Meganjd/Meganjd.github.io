Hello!

I’m Megan. From a young age I was always experimenting with the arts, through painting,drawing, many failed attempts at sculpture and a piano lesson or two. Today, my passions lay in Illustration, User Experience Design, Brand Identity, Print Media, Typography and Package Design. When I’m not painting, doodling or designing you can find me out for a run with my dog or enjoying some veggie pizza with a beer in hand, usually in that order.
