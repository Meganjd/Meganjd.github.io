Hello!

I’m Megan. From a young age I was always experimenting with the arts, through painting,drawing, many failed attempts at sculpture and a piano lesson or two. Today, my passions lay in Illustration, User Experience Design, Brand Identity, Print Media, Typography and Package Design. When I’m not painting, doodling or designing you can find me out for a run with my dog or enjoying some veggie pizza with a beer in hand, usually in that order.

My Work.

Fruit Posters & Icons

These icons were originally created as dingbats for a typography project. I then took them and developed them more. I came up with the idea of using them for a healthy eating poster/flyer series. Ideally these posters would be placed around universities and colleges reminding students to choose healthier foods, letting them know what the benefits are, in a relatable way.

Tazo Tea Packaging

This was my very first packaging product and I had a lot of fun with it. I wanted to create something for tazo tea that wasn't just your regular box. This packaging was made to emulate the form of a teabag. For each flavour of tea, what is shown here as green, would change to suit the corresponding flavour.

Nama-stay App

Nama-stay is an iOs app for everyone interested in taking yoga classes. Whether you're a well conditioned yogi, or you just like to do a couple drop in classes when you have the time. The app provides you with the ability to look at and check-in to classes in yoga studios near you.

Stella Luna Rebrand

This piece is a school rebranding project. The goal was to create a new identity for a local company with a less desirable logo. I chose to rebrand Stella Luna Gelato, a great little family owned business in Ottawa! The concept behind my design was to incorproate the crescent moon symbol, as well as gelato itself, so I found a way to meld the two together. I chose raspberry, and espresso for the two colours in the logo.

Elephant Painting

In my spare time I really enjoy getting away from the laptop and using a paintbrush to create. It’s nice to get back to the basics with my art. This painting I completed in 2012. There were about 9 hours total put into this piece. Elephants have been one of my favourite animals every since I can remember, hence the inspiration behind this painting. This was created on a 22 x 28 inch canvas using acrylic paint.

Wu-tang Clan Event Poster

I drew this poster for a theoretical event. Wu-tang clan is one of my all time favourite muscial groups. The majority of this poster is done by hand, exceot of course for the ticket, time and location typography at the bottom. I wanted to give the poster a fun feel, while also keeping it a bit gangsta.
